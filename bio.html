Speaker bio

Anna Shipman is Chief Technology Officer at Kooth, a company specialising in digital mental health.

She has been a software engineer for 20 years. Before working at Kooth, she was Technical Director for Customer Products at the Financial Times, leading on the FT.com website and the apps. Peviously she was a Technical Architect at the UK's Government Digital Service, launching the GOV.UK website.

She speaks at conferences, blogs on her personal website, very occasionally tweets and is always up for a game of pool.
