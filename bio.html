Speaker bio

Anna Shipman is Technical Director for Customer Products at the Financial Times, leading on B2C products including the award-winning FT.com website and the FT iPhone and Android apps.

She has been a software developer for nearly 20 years. Before working at the FT, she was a Technical Architect at the UK's Government Digital Service leading on Open Source, GOV.UK PaaS and GOV.UK.

She speaks at conferences, blogs on her personal website, tweets and is always up for a game of pool.
