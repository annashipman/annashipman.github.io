Speaker bio

Anna Shipman is Technical Director for Customer Products at the Financial Times, leading on the award-winning FT.com website and the FT iPhone and Android apps.

Prior to that, she was the UK government's Open Source Lead at the Government Digital Service.

She is an international speaker. She blogs, tweets and is always up for a game of pool.
